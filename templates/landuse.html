Modeling Land use changes

Changes in land use and climate, both individually and in combination, have profound implication for rural lives and livelihoods.

The combined imacts of climate and land use change are numerous. Most importantly, the depletion of natural resources that crop and livestock depend to survive increases the risk of conflict due to competion over limitted resources and eventually the displacement of people and loss of lives.

Modeling how land use changes over time and space has benefits to strengthen the overall analysis of forced displacement as well as independently serving as a guide to detect areas that could exprience ecological disturbance overtime
