About me

Born and raised in rural Ethiopia, I have been watching as my country makes the headlines on conflict and climate change induced disasters especially in recent years. Hence, for me, there is nothing like working on projects that reduce the impact of natural and manmade disasters. This include saving the lives and livelihoods of the affected populations as well as supporting them to survive and thrive in post disaster situation.

With background in data science and international development, I focus on researching the interplay between conflict, displacement and climate change. I am currently working on projects that broadly adress the following questions:

  • What is the probability of conflict at sub-national level?
  • What is the pattern and magnitude of forced displacement within and across countries?
  • What is the relative importances of the causes of conflict and displacement?
