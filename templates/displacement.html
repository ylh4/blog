Modeling Forced Displacement

Displaced populations experience terrible circomstances including extremely stressful events such as operation, the loss of loved ones, distruction of poperty, hunger, rape, torture; to name a few. Assisting the ffected populations gives me a fulfilment in my professional career as I do not want to see the suffering to happen to anyone.

As of 2020, 1 person is forced to leave their home every two seconds often with nothing but clothes on their back due to the plight of conflict, climate change or other disasters. This makes tens of millions of newly displaced people every year.

Therefore, I believe that insights into the issue such as how a forced displacement will evolve over time and space has importance in supporting humanitarian and development decisions in many countries.
