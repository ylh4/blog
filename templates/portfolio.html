Analysis and Modeling

I am dedicated to harnessing the field of data science to unpack pressing issues that affect peace and development. My current projects include modeling forced displacement, conflict, and land-use changes primarly in East African countries.

As we were leaving the camp, I wondered whether refugee and IDP camps are a sign of compassion towards displaced people, or are they signs of how far humans have gone in causing harm to each other?

Louis Yako in goodreads.com

The interplay between conflict, displacement and climate change are my research interests. I also acknowledge the challenge in terms of having a testable hypothesis to study the issue of forced displacement as a whole due to the existance of multi-directional relationship. For simplicity, I am taking a direct apporach to modeling each trends separately and find ways of integrating the models along the way. My current projects adress the following questions:

  • What is the pattern and magnitude of forced displacement?
  • What is the probability of conflict ?
  • What is the role of land-use change as a displacement?
